Annual Food Drive for Families in Need

June 15, 2025
Community Center, Main Street

About This Event

Our annual food drive aims to collect and distribute essential food items to families facing hardship in our community. Last year, we helped over 500 families with nutritious meals during difficult times.

This year, we're expanding our efforts to reach even more families. We need volunteers to help with collection, sorting, and distribution of food items. All donated items will be distributed through local food banks and community organizations.

What to Bring

  • Non-perishable food items (canned goods, rice, pasta, etc.)
  • Personal hygiene products
  • Volunteers should bring water bottles and wear comfortable clothing

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Food distribution to families
